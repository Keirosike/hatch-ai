How It Works

From setup to hatch prediction

HatchAI simplifies incubation with smart automation, real-time monitoring, and AI-powered predictions for better hatch results.

Step 1

Insert Eggs

Place eggs into the organized trays inside the incubator for stable spacing and airflow.

Step 2

Monitor Conditions

Sensors track temperature and humidity in real time to keep your incubation environment visible.

Step 3

Automatic Control

The system automatically adjusts heat and environment using relay-controlled heating.

Step 4

Egg Turning System

Motorized trays rotate eggs automatically at timed intervals for even development.

Step 5

AI Prediction

HatchAI predicts hatch day and confidence level, then displays insights directly on the LCD.
